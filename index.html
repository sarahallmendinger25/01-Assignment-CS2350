UX, UI, and You

While you are starting your journey in becoming a programmer, the possiblities of potential careers seem endless! With the rise of new and exciting technological developments, the world is in need of more skilled applicants to fill these roles.

Within these skilled roles are those who fill the positions of User Experience Designers (UX) and User Interface Designers (UI).

What is the Difference Between UX and UI Roles?

One of the key differences between the definitions of these roles is that a User Experience career can expand beyond digital role and be used for physical design of a product, while a User Interface career is specifically a digital-based field.These roles go hand-in-hand, however. The UX designer will determine what the client is looking for in an experience, and the UI designer will implement the functionality and style of the interface.
